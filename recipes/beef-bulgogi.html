Beef Bulgogi

Description:

TBulgogi, sometimes known as Korean BBQ beef, is a dish of thinly sliced grilled steak that has been marinated in a sweet soy, sesame, and garlic sauce. If you want to spice it up, serve the beef in lettuce cups with rice and hot pepper paste (gochujang).

Ingredients

  • Flank steak (Can be substituted for skirt steak or hanger steak)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Green onion and garlic
  • Sesame seeds
  • Sesame oil
  • Pepper
